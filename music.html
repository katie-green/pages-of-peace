Frequencies

White noise has always been known as the soft static sound a radio or televistion emits. Pink noise is a lower frequency, often associated with rainfall or traffic.
Brown noise mimics a type of erratic pattern called "Brownian Motion", named after Scotish botanist, Robert Brown. While evidence is still being gathered on its effectiveness, its incredibly low frequency has been proving to highly reduce stress.
This page introduces these different frequencies and how they can enhance and benefit your life.
